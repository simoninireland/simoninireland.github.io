    Pittenweem

    Pittenweem is one of the Fife fishing villages on the Firth of Forth, the only remaining landing port for the fishing fleet of the East Neuk of Fife. It gets the most amazing skyscapes and sunsets, as well as the occasional autumn storm that brings waves breaking over the pier.
