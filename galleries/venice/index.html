Venice (2017)

Venice, perhaps the most romantic city in Europe, and spectacular (and packed!) at New Year. Some of these shots have been given a 1950's treatment, as that seems to capture the feeling of the light and the canals and the buildings better than either ordinary colour or pure black & white.
