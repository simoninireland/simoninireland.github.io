* Consulting work

  Simon I have worked in computer science for over thirty years in
  academia, government, and start-ups. I work with large software
  companies, infrastructure providers, and start-ups on projects that
  make use of sensors and data analytics, helping organisations
  understand what AI- and data-driven processes and technologies can
  bring to their operations and how to choose and deploy them
  successfully.

  Trained as a scientist and engineer, I always views problems as
  systems to be built: what do we need to know, how do we collect that
  data, how do we make use of what we learn, and how do we embed this
  learning into robust, scalable, and long-lived solutions? I've
  gained huge experience in strategic planning and systems design, as
  well as with a huge range of technology ranging from mature to
  cutting-edge.

  I am a Chartered Engineer and Fellow of the British Computer
  Society, as well as being a Fellow of the Royal Society of
  Edinburgh.
